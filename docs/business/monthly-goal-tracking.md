# Monthly Goal Tracking

The business target is $3,000-$5,000/month.

## MRR Calculation

Monthly recurring revenue is the sum of converted offers with monthly billing.

## One-Time Revenue Calculation

One-time revenue is counted for conversions in the current calendar month.

## Goal Progress

`revenue-summary.md` reports progress toward:

- $3,000/month
- $5,000/month

It also estimates clients still needed at $500/month, $1,000/month, and $1,500/month.

## Recommended Weekly Revenue Review

1. Run `npm run revenue:summary`.
2. Review active monthly offers.
3. Review one-time offers sold this month.
4. Review top revenue opportunities.
5. Convert won leads immediately.
6. Close low-fit or dead leads so daily follow-up stays focused.
