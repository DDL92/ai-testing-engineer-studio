# Qualification Framework

Score each lead from 0-10. Use public information and manual judgment only.

## Scoring Checklist

### Website Quality: 0-2 Points

- 0: No usable website or only a placeholder.
- 1: Website exists but product workflow is unclear.
- 2: Website clearly shows a web product, app, store, marketplace, booking flow, or portal.

### Product Complexity: 0-2 Points

- 0: Static brochure site.
- 1: Some interactive behavior or forms.
- 2: Multiple workflows such as signup, login, checkout, booking, dashboard, integrations, or roles.

### Revenue Potential: 0-1 Point

- 0: No clear commercial value or very small project.
- 1: Product reliability likely affects revenue, retention, or client delivery.

### Automation Potential: 0-2 Points

- 0: No obvious safe automation target.
- 1: One public workflow could become a smoke test.
- 2: Multiple safe workflows could become Playwright coverage.

### Retainer Potential: 0-1 Point

- 0: One-time audit only.
- 1: Recurring releases, agency clients, or ongoing maintenance likely.

### Access Difficulty: 0-1 Point

- 0: Requires risky production credentials, payment testing, or private data.
- 1: Public or staging-safe workflow can be reviewed first.

### Technical Fit: 0-1 Point

- 0: Poor fit for Playwright/web QA automation.
- 1: Good fit for Playwright, smoke testing, CI checks, or QA reporting.

## Score Interpretation

- 0-3: Not fit or pause.
- 4-5: Possible QA Audit only.
- 6-7: Good Playwright Starter Pack candidate.
- 8-10: Strong retainer or agency partner candidate.

## Required Notes

For every qualified lead, record:

- Why selected
- Potential pain points
- Recommended offer
- Manual next action
- Red flags
