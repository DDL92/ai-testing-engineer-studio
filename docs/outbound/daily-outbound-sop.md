# Daily Outbound SOP

This is a 30-minute daily process for manual, evidence-led outbound. The goal is to add 3-5 qualified leads per day, not to send high-volume outreach.

## Daily Process

### 10 Minutes: Review Cockpit

Run or review:

```sh
npm run cockpit
```

Check:

- Today's revenue focus
- Top leads
- Active clients
- Revenue snapshot
- Generated files
- Next manual actions

Decision:

- Which lead deserves review today?
- Which client/report needs attention first?
- Is there a lead ready for an audit or SOW?

### 10 Minutes: Research Leads

Manually research 3-5 leads.

Capture:

- Company
- Website
- Industry
- Contact
- Why selected
- Potential pain points
- Initial score

Research only public information. Do not scrape, enrich private contact data, or use credentials.

### 10 Minutes: Prepare Outreach Drafts

Prepare 1-3 message drafts manually.

Use:

- Lead score
- Lead pack
- Audit angle
- Clear offer fit
- Human approval checklist

Do not send during the drafting step unless Daniel explicitly approves the final message.

## Daily Output

Target output:

- 3-5 qualified leads/day
- 1-3 reviewed message drafts/day
- 0 automated sends
- 0 scraped contact lists
- 1 clear next revenue action

## Quality Bar

A lead is useful only if it has:

- A real website or product workflow
- A plausible QA pain point
- A likely buyer or decision maker
- A realistic offer fit
- A clear manual next action

## Stop Conditions

Stop and review before continuing if:

- A workflow would require credentials
- A message would be sent automatically
- A production client system would be tested
- The lead requires private data enrichment
- The task starts turning into mass outreach
