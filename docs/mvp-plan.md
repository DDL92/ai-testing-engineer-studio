# MVP Plan

## Executive Summary

AI Testing Engineer Studio is a service-first MVP for selling QA Automation and AI Testing work. The first version focuses on client acquisition, repeatable delivery, and portfolio credibility.

## What The MVP Includes

- Three clear offers: Playwright audit, Playwright starter framework, AI testing audit.
- A reusable Playwright + TypeScript framework.
- AI testing mini-kit for prompt, RAG, safety, and response quality checks.
- Client delivery templates for intake, reports, handoff, and retainers.
- Upwork profile, project catalog, and proposal assets.
- Codex prompts to speed up delivery.
- Ten initial content ideas and seven-day launch plan.

## What Stays Out

- SaaS product.
- Complex dashboard.
- Paid course.
- Large ebook.
- Advanced AI evaluation platform.
- Overly generic agency site.
- Complex CRM automation.

## 7-Day Objective

Launch a professional repo, publish three Upwork Project Catalog offers, post initial content, and send targeted outreach to at least 20 prospects.

## 30-Day Objective

Close one paid audit, deliver it with the templates, convert the best lead into a starter framework or $750+ monthly retainer, and package one small digital asset.

## First Deliverables

- One QA Automation Audit report.
- One coverage matrix.
- One mini Playwright smoke test suite.
- One CI/CD recommendation.
- One next-step proposal.

## Ideal First Client

A small SaaS, AI app, agency, or funded startup with manual regression pain, no stable automation framework, and a web app with clear critical flows.

## How To Get The First Sale

1. Publish the three Upwork catalog offers.
2. Send 20 direct proposals to SaaS and AI app jobs.
3. Post proof-based content showing audit examples.
4. Offer a fixed-scope audit with fast delivery.
5. Convert audit findings into a framework proposal.

## Do Not Build Yet

- SaaS.
- Community.
- Full course.
- Complex newsletter funnel.
- Custom reporting app.
- Large prompt database.
