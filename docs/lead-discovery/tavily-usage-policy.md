# Tavily Usage Policy

Sprint 14 focuses Tavily/API/search spend on commercial buyer-intent discovery for active or potential paying clients. Flora and Fauna Foods is the explicit top priority because Flora has expressed strong interest, willingness to pay, and is waiting to be contacted.

## Priority Order

1. Flora and Fauna Foods
2. LZT Costa Rica
3. Costa Retreats
4. Future paying clients

## Allowed Use

- Buyer-intent searches only.
- Recency-first searches that can reasonably produce a deliverable lead.
- Client-specific searches tied to Flora, LZT, Costa, or another active/potential paying client.
- Quality-over-volume searches with a clear commercial hypothesis.
- Manual review before delivery, client-facing use, or any contact.

## Prohibited Use

- No Tavily credits for old QA audit discovery.
- No Tavily credits for website audit discovery.
- No Tavily credits for generic Studio experiments.
- No broad research without an active commercial client.
- No non-commercial searches.
- No outreach, emails, calls, DMs, forms, or auto-contact.
- No contact extraction.
- No scraping, crawling, browser automation, or login flows.

## Budget Philosophy

Do not spend Tavily credits on searches that cannot realistically produce deliverable leads.

Every Tavily query must answer:

> Can this query reasonably generate a lead that we can sell or deliver to Flora, LZT, Costa, or another active client?

If the answer is no, do not run it.

The north star is delivered leads that create real conversations, not the number of leads found.
