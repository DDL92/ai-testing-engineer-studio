# AI Lead Discovery Studio Verticals

The first verticals share the same lead model but use different intent signals and sales angles.

## travel_leads

- Ideal buyer: travel agencies, tour operators, destination planners, retreat planners, boutique hospitality teams.
- Lead intent signals: asks for itinerary help, honeymoon planning, family trip ideas, group retreat planning, local guide recommendations, specific destination dates.
- Public/free source ideas: public travel forums, destination groups with public posts, review-site questions, public event boards, tourism inquiry pages.
- Fields needed: destination, origin if public, travel dates, group size, trip type, budget range, source URL, urgency, risk flags.
- Example sales angle: “This person is planning a Costa Rica family trip for specific dates and asked for guided itinerary help.”

## catering_leads

- Ideal buyer: caterers, private chefs, event food vendors, venue partners.
- Lead intent signals: asks for catering, chef, menu, dietary accommodations, guest count, event date, venue, budget.
- Public/free source ideas: public event groups, wedding/vendor forums, community boards, venue inquiry posts, public marketplace requests.
- Fields needed: event type, event date, guest count, location, service style, dietary needs, budget range, source URL.
- Example sales angle: “This organizer needs buffet catering for 80 guests next month and already shared a location.”

## wedding_leads

- Ideal buyer: wedding planners, venues, photographers, florists, caterers, DJs.
- Lead intent signals: vendor search, venue availability, wedding date, guest count, budget, destination wedding request.
- Public/free source ideas: public wedding forums, destination wedding groups, venue comment threads, public vendor directories with inquiry context.
- Fields needed: wedding date, location, guest count, vendor category, budget signal, urgency, source URL.
- Example sales angle: “This couple has a confirmed wedding month and is still missing venue and catering vendors.”

## real_estate_leads

- Ideal buyer: real estate agents, relocation consultants, property managers, buyer representatives.
- Lead intent signals: moving date, target area, property type, rental/buying intent, budget, financing readiness.
- Public/free source ideas: public relocation forums, community groups, public real estate Q&A pages, expat boards.
- Fields needed: target location, buy/rent intent, timeline, budget, property type, household needs, source URL.
- Example sales angle: “This relocating buyer has a target neighborhood and move timeline but needs local property guidance.”

## website_leads

- Ideal buyer: website studios, local marketing agencies, freelance web designers, conversion specialists.
- Lead intent signals: broken site, missing booking path, outdated website, public request for website help, business launch needing web presence.
- Public/free source ideas: public business directories, public social posts asking for web help, chamber listings, public local business pages.
- Fields needed: business name, website URL, issue summary, contactability, source URL, risk flags, verified opportunity.
- Example sales angle: “This business has a public booking offer but no clear conversion path on the website.”

## qa_leads

- Ideal buyer: QA consultants, Playwright automation providers, SaaS testing vendors.
- Lead intent signals: hiring QA, public bug complaints, release-quality concerns, test automation hiring, app reliability issues.
- Public/free source ideas: public job posts, public issue trackers, status pages, product reviews, release notes, public support forums.
- Fields needed: company, product, QA trigger, source URL, technical buyer context, urgency, risk flags.
- Example sales angle: “This SaaS team is hiring for test automation and has recent release-quality signals.”
