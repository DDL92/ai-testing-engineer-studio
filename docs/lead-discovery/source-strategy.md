# AI Lead Discovery Studio Source Strategy

This sprint defines source planning only. It does not scrape, call paid APIs, buy lead databases, or automate outreach. Every future lead must remain subject to human review before delivery or contact.

## travel_leads

- Ideal buyer: travel planners, destination agencies, retreat planners, boutique tour operators.
- Lead intent signals: trip planning, destination comparison, honeymoon planning, surf trips, retreat logistics, private transfers.
- Public intent phrases:
  - "planning trip to Costa Rica"
  - "looking for honeymoon recommendations"
  - "surf trip Costa Rica"
  - "need help planning itinerary"
- Possible public sources: public travel forums, public destination Q&A pages, public retreat/event boards, tourism community boards.
- Risks: personal travel details, stale posts, low budget clarity, public forum terms, duplicate posts.
- Recommended scoring factors: destination match, travel date, group size, budget/luxury signal, itinerary specificity, safe public contact path.

## catering_leads

- Ideal buyer: caterers, private chefs, event food vendors, venue partners.
- Lead intent signals: event catering request, guest count, date, venue/location, dietary needs, corporate lunch, wedding catering.
- Public intent phrases:
  - "need catering for 100 people"
  - "corporate lunch recommendations"
  - "wedding catering suggestions"
  - "private chef for dinner party"
- Possible public sources: public event boards, community recommendation posts, public wedding/vendor forums, venue partner directories.
- Risks: event details may be incomplete, budget often unstated, duplicate vendor requests, posts may receive many replies.
- Recommended scoring factors: event date, guest count, service style, location match, budget signal, contactability.

## wedding_leads

- Ideal buyer: wedding planners, venues, caterers, photographers, florists, DJs.
- Lead intent signals: wedding date, destination wedding, guest count, vendor gaps, budget range, venue search.
- Public intent phrases:
  - "looking for wedding vendors"
  - "destination wedding planner recommendations"
  - "need venue and catering"
  - "wedding photographer available"
- Possible public sources: public wedding forums, vendor recommendation groups, destination wedding boards, public venue Q&A pages.
- Risks: private life event context, duplicate requests, vendor spam, unclear budget.
- Recommended scoring factors: confirmed date, location, guest count, missing vendor category, budget signal, public source quality.

## real_estate_leads

- Ideal buyer: real estate agents, relocation consultants, property managers, buyer representatives.
- Lead intent signals: moving timeline, target location, rent/buy intent, neighborhood comparison, school/commute needs.
- Public intent phrases:
  - "moving to Austin before school starts"
  - "looking for rental near downtown"
  - "best neighborhoods for families"
  - "need realtor recommendation"
- Possible public sources: public relocation forums, community Q&A boards, public expat boards, real estate recommendation threads.
- Risks: housing privacy, location sensitivity, stale posts, financing unknown, regulated-market claims.
- Recommended scoring factors: timeline, location specificity, buy/rent clarity, property type, budget/rent signal, safe handoff notes.

## website_leads

- Ideal buyer: website studios, local marketing agencies, conversion specialists.
- Lead intent signals: businesses with no website, outdated website, poor mobile experience, missing booking page, public request for website help.
- Public intent phrases:
  - "businesses with no website"
  - "outdated website"
  - "poor mobile experience"
  - "need booking page"
- Possible public sources: public business directories, chamber listings, public local business posts, public recommendations for web designers.
- Risks: false positives from old/migrated domains, static inspection gaps, unclear official website, weak opportunity signals.
- Recommended scoring factors: verified current site, conversion path, mobile risk, contactability, business activity, no migrated-domain false positive.

## qa_leads

- Ideal buyer: QA consultants, Playwright automation providers, SaaS testing vendors.
- Lead intent signals: startups hiring QA, product launches, rapid growth companies, public bug complaints, release pressure.
- Public intent phrases:
  - "startups hiring QA"
  - "product launch QA help"
  - "Playwright automation contractor"
  - "need test automation before launch"
- Possible public sources: public job boards, public GitHub issues, public release notes, status pages, public product feedback forums.
- Risks: enterprise low-fit targets, old hiring posts, unclear buyer, technical issue context may be incomplete.
- Recommended scoring factors: recent QA hiring, launch timing, product complexity, public quality signal, company size/fit, contact verification.
