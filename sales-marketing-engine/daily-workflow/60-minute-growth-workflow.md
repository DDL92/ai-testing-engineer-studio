# 60-Minute Growth Workflow

## 10 Min: CRM

- Review due follow-ups.
- Update statuses.
- Identify highest-value active leads.

## 15 Min: Lead Research

- Research 8-10 leads.
- Prioritize SaaS, AI apps, agencies, and QA automation jobs.
- Add qualified leads to CRM.

## 15 Min: Proposals

- Send 5 personalized proposals/messages.
- Use the correct service template.
- Ask one smart question.

## 15 Min: Content

- Repurpose one repo file into a LinkedIn post, X thread, or carousel.
- Tie content to one service.
- Add soft CTA.

## 5 Min: Dashboard Update

- Update weekly metrics.
- Note which message or platform performed best.
