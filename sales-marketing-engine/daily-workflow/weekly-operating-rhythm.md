# Weekly Operating Rhythm

## Monday

- Lead sourcing.
- Post content.
- Add 10 new leads.

## Tuesday

- Send proposals.
- Send follow-ups.
- Improve one proposal based on replies.

## Wednesday

- Repurpose content.
- LinkedIn outreach.
- Review reply quality.

## Thursday

- Proposal review.
- Offer testing.
- Send pricing or scope clarifications.

## Friday

- Weekly dashboard.
- CRM cleanup.
- Review wins, objections, and next actions.

## Weekend Optional

- Batch content.
- Update portfolio assets.
- Improve one template.
