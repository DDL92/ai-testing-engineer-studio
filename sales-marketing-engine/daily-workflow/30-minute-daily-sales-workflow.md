# 30-Minute Daily Sales Workflow

## 5 Min: CRM Review

- Open `crm-tracker/leads.csv`.
- Check follow-ups due today.
- Identify top 3 priority leads.

## 10 Min: Lead Research

- Find 5 SaaS, AI app, agency, or QA automation leads.
- Qualify each lead quickly.
- Add only relevant leads to CRM.

## 10 Min: Proposal/Outreach Generation

- Send 3-5 personalized messages or proposals.
- Use one concrete personalization detail.
- Match each lead to one offer.

## 3 Min: Follow-Up

- Send one Day 2, Day 5, or final follow-up.
- Keep it short and respectful.

## 2 Min: Tracking

- Update status.
- Add last contact date.
- Add next follow-up date.
- Add notes.
