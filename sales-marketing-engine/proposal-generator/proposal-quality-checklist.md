# Proposal Quality Checklist

Before sending, confirm:

- [ ] Custom first line references the client/job.
- [ ] Clear client pain point is named.
- [ ] Relevant service is pitched.
- [ ] Specific deliverables are included.
- [ ] Timeline is realistic.
- [ ] Price or price range is clear when appropriate.
- [ ] No overpromising.
- [ ] Strong CTA is included.
- [ ] One or two smart questions are included.
- [ ] Portfolio link is included when relevant.
- [ ] No generic phrases like "I am perfect for this job."
- [ ] Proposal is under the required word count.
- [ ] Tone is senior, direct, practical, and not desperate.
