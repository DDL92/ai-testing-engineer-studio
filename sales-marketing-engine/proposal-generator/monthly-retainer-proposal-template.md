# Monthly QA Automation Retainer Proposal Template

Service: Monthly QA Automation Retainer  
Plans: $750 / $1,500 / $2,500 per month

## Starter Plan: $750/month

Best fit: Small team with existing tests or a small app that needs basic monthly support.

Included:

- Up to 5 new or updated tests.
- Flaky test review.
- CI failure triage.
- Monthly QA summary.
- Async support.

## Growth Plan: $1,500/month

Best fit: SaaS team actively expanding Playwright coverage.

Included:

- Up to 12 new or updated tests.
- UI and API smoke coverage.
- Coverage matrix updates.
- Bug-to-test conversion.
- One monthly review call.

## Premium Plan: $2,500/month

Best fit: AI app or fast-moving SaaS team needing UI, API, and AI regression coverage.

Included:

- Up to 20 new or updated tests.
- Playwright UI/API coverage.
- AI response quality checks.
- Defensive prompt injection checks.
- Weekly async QA summary.
- Two monthly review calls.

## What Is Not Included

- Full-time QA availability.
- Manual testing for every release.
- Production credential handling outside secure client-approved methods.
- Large framework rewrites without separate scope.
- Security penetration testing.

## CTA

I recommend starting with the Growth plan for 30 days to stabilize critical flows and measure regression coverage improvement.

## Renewal Note

Retainers renew monthly. Scope can be adjusted after the first month based on test stability, release frequency, and coverage goals.
