# Sales Metrics

Track these weekly.

## Activity Metrics

- Leads researched.
- Qualified leads.
- Outreach messages sent.
- Follow-ups sent.
- Proposals sent.
- Content posts published.

## Conversion Metrics

- Reply rate = replies / messages sent.
- Call booking rate = calls booked / replies.
- Proposal rate = proposals sent / calls or qualified replies.
- Close rate = closed deals / proposals.
- Audit-to-framework conversion = framework deals / audits delivered.
- Retainer conversion = retainers / completed projects.

## Revenue Metrics

- Revenue booked.
- Revenue collected.
- Average project value.
- Retainer monthly recurring revenue.

## Weekly Targets

- 25 leads researched.
- 15 qualified leads.
- 20 personalized outreach messages.
- 5 replies.
- 2 calls or async scoping conversations.
- 1 proposal.
- 1 paid audit or active opportunity.
