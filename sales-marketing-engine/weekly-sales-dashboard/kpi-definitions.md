# KPI Definitions

## Lead

Definition: A person or company that could buy one service.  
Formula: Count of new rows added to CRM.  
Why it matters: Measures pipeline input.  
Target range: 25+ per week.

## Qualified Lead

Definition: Lead with clear fit, pain, and service match.  
Formula: Qualified leads / total leads.  
Why it matters: Measures targeting quality.  
Target range: 50%+.

## Contacted Lead

Definition: Lead that received a manual message or proposal.  
Formula: Count of leads with `Contacted` or later status.  
Why it matters: Research does not create revenue until outreach happens.  
Target range: 20+ per week.

## Reply Rate

Definition: Percentage of contacted leads that reply.  
Formula: responses / messages sent.  
Why it matters: Shows message and targeting quality.  
Target range: 10%-25%.

## Call Booking Rate

Definition: Percentage of replies that become calls or async scoping conversations.  
Formula: calls booked / responses.  
Why it matters: Shows whether replies are moving forward.  
Target range: 20%-50%.

## Proposal Rate

Definition: Percentage of calls or qualified replies that receive a proposal.  
Formula: proposals sent / qualified replies or calls.  
Why it matters: Shows sales process progression.  
Target range: 30%-60%.

## Close Rate

Definition: Percentage of proposals that become paid work.  
Formula: deals won / proposals sent.  
Why it matters: Shows offer and pricing effectiveness.  
Target range: 20%-40%.

## Average Deal Size

Definition: Average revenue per closed deal.  
Formula: revenue closed / deals won.  
Why it matters: Shows whether sales are moving beyond small audits.  
Target range: $500-$1,500 early.

## Revenue Closed

Definition: Confirmed paid revenue for the period.  
Formula: Sum of won deal values.  
Why it matters: Measures actual business progress.  
Target range: grows from $500 to $5,000/month.

## Pipeline Value

Definition: Estimated value of active interested/proposal leads.  
Formula: Sum estimated_value for Interested, Call Booked, and Proposal Sent.  
Why it matters: Shows future revenue potential.  
Target range: 3x monthly revenue goal.

## Follow-Up Completion Rate

Definition: Percentage of due follow-ups completed.  
Formula: follow-ups sent / follow-ups due.  
Why it matters: Most deals require more than one touch.  
Target range: 90%+.
