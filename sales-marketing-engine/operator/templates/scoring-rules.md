# Lead Scoring Rules

Leads are scored from 1 to 10.

## Positive Signals

- Role includes Founder, CTO, QA Manager, Engineering Manager, or Head of QA.
- Company type includes SaaS, AI app, software agency, or startup.
- Pain point includes automation, regression, QA, release, testing, Playwright, Cypress, or AI.
- Service fit is one of the four core offers.
- Estimated value is $500 or higher.
- Priority is High or Medium.

## Score Interpretation

- 8-10: Contact today.
- 6-7: Qualified but needs personalization.
- 4-5: Keep in CRM, research more.
- 1-3: Low priority or poor fit.

## Recommended Service Mapping

- Unclear QA pain: Playwright QA Automation Audit.
- Framework setup need: Playwright Starter Framework.
- AI quality/safety concern: AI Testing Audit.
- Ongoing test work: Monthly QA Automation Retainer.
