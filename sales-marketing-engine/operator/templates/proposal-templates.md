Medium: I recommend starting with a Playwright QA Automation Audit before building a large framework. ## AI Testing Audit

Medium: I can review your AI workflow using representative prompts, expected outputs, quality scoring, grounding checks, and safe defensive prompt injection tests. The output is a practical report, reusable test matrix, response quality rubric, and regression plan.
