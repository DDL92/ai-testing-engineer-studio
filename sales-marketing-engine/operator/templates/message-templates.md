# Message Templates

## Personalization Rules

- Mention one concrete company, product, role, or pain detail.
- Match the message to one service.
- Keep first messages short.
- Ask for permission before sending details.
- Never imply automated scanning or scraping.

- Hi [Name], I work on Playwright QA automation and AI Testing for SaaS teams. I saw [Company] and wanted to connect.
- Hi [Name], I help teams reduce regression risk with Playwright + TypeScript testing. Your work at [Company] looks relevant.
- Hi [Name], I focus on QA automation audits and AI testing for SaaS and AI apps. Wanted to connect.
