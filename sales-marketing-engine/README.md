# Sales & Marketing Automation Engine

This is a lightweight sales preparation, personalization, follow-up, and tracking system for AI Testing Engineer Studio.

It is not a spam automation system. It does not scrape leads, auto-send LinkedIn messages, auto-apply to Upwork jobs, or replace human judgment. It helps prepare better research, outreach, proposals, content, follow-ups, and weekly reviews so every message can be sent manually and responsibly.

## Who This Is For

- QA Automation freelancers selling B2B services.
- Playwright + TypeScript specialists building a service pipeline.
- AI Testing consultants promoting audits and retainers.
- Technical founders who need a simple sales operating system.

## Services This Helps Sell

1. Playwright QA Automation Audit: $300-$500, 2-3 days.
2. Playwright Starter Framework: $800-$1,500, 5-7 days.
3. AI Testing Audit: $500-$1,000, 3-5 days.
4. Monthly QA Automation Retainer: $750, $1,500, or $2,500/month.

## What It Automates

- Lead research prompts.
- Proposal drafting.
- Outreach message preparation.
- Follow-up wording.
- Content repurposing.
- CRM tracking structure.
- Weekly sales dashboard templates.

## What It Does Not Automate

- No spam sending.
- No LinkedIn bots.
- No Upwork automation.
- No aggressive scraping.
- No paid tools required.
- No fake personalization.

## Daily Workflow

1. Review CRM and follow-ups due.
2. Research 5 leads manually.
3. Qualify leads before outreach.
4. Send 3-5 personalized messages or proposals.
5. Track status and next follow-up date.
6. Repurpose one repo asset into content when possible.

## Weekly Workflow

1. Review leads added, messages sent, replies, calls, proposals, and wins.
2. Identify which platform performed best.
3. Identify which service created the most interest.
4. Improve one message and one proposal template.
5. Plan next week's sales activity.

## Folder Map

- `crm-tracker`: leads CSV, statuses, CRM usage, weekly CRM review.
- `proposal-generator`: proposal prompts, service templates, pricing guidance.
- `outreach-templates`: LinkedIn, Upwork, email, replies, objections, Spanish templates.
- `content-repurposing-system`: prompts for LinkedIn, Instagram, X, video, calendar, CTAs.
- `weekly-sales-dashboard`: metrics, dashboard, KPI definitions, revenue plan.
- `daily-workflow`: 30-minute and 60-minute operating workflows.

## How This Supports $3k-$5k/Month

The system prioritizes a low-risk entry offer first: the Playwright QA Automation Audit. A few audits create proof, client trust, and natural upsells into Starter Frameworks or monthly retainers.

Example paths:

- $3,000/month: two $1,200 frameworks + one $750 retainer.
- $5,000/month: two $1,500 retainers + two $1,000 AI audits.
- Starter path: four $500 audits, then convert one client into a $1,500 framework.
