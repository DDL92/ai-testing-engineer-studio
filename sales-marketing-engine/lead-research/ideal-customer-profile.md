# Ideal Customer Profile

## Best-Fit Customers

### 1. Small SaaS Team

Signals:

- Web app with login, dashboard, billing, reports, or admin workflows.
- Manual regression before releases.
- No clear Playwright framework.
- Hiring QA automation help or posting testing tasks.

Best first offer: Playwright QA Automation Audit.

### 2. AI Product Team

Signals:

- Chatbot, RAG app, AI workflow, or AI agent.
- Response quality complaints.
- Prompt changes without regression tests.
- Need to validate grounding, format, or safety.

Best first offer: AI Testing Audit.

### 3. Startup Preparing Releases

Signals:

- Frequent shipping.
- Small team.
- Bugs reaching users.
- Need CI-ready smoke coverage.

Best first offer: Playwright Starter Framework.

### 4. Agency With Client Delivery

Signals:

- Builds SaaS or web apps for clients.
- Needs repeatable QA automation support.
- Wants contractor help without hiring full-time.

Best first offer: Monthly QA Automation Retainer.

## Poor-Fit Customers

- No web application.
- No staging or test environment.
- Wants only manual testing at very low budget.
- Wants bulk spam, scraping, or unsafe AI testing.
- Cannot provide scope, access, or success criteria.

## Qualification Score

Score each lead from 1-5:

- 5: Strong fit, clear pain, budget likely, specific project.
- 4: Good fit, needs light discovery.
- 3: Possible fit, unclear timing or budget.
- 2: Weak fit, limited pain or access.
- 1: Not worth outreach now.
