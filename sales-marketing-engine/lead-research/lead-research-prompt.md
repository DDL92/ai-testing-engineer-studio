# Lead Research Prompt

## Use

Use this prompt manually with a company website, Upwork job post, LinkedIn profile, or product page.

## Prompt

Act as a Senior QA Automation Sales Research Assistant. Analyze this company or job post and decide whether they are a good fit for AI Testing Engineer Studio.

Services available:

- Playwright QA Automation Audit
- Playwright Starter Framework
- AI Testing Audit
- Monthly QA Automation Retainer

Research input:

```text
Paste company website, job post, product notes, or LinkedIn profile notes here.
```

Return:

1. Company/product summary.
2. Likely QA pain points.
3. Best service to offer first.
4. Why this lead is qualified or not qualified.
5. Personalization angle.
6. Suggested first message.
7. Suggested follow-up.
8. Risk of bad fit.

Rules:

- Do not invent facts.
- Clearly separate evidence from assumptions.
- Keep the first message under 90 words.
- Avoid pushy or spammy language.
