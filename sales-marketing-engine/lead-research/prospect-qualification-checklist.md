# Prospect Qualification Checklist

Use this before sending outreach.

## Company Fit

- [ ] Has a web app, SaaS product, AI feature, or client delivery workflow.
- [ ] Has users who would be impacted by regressions.
- [ ] Likely ships changes regularly.
- [ ] Has enough complexity to benefit from automation.

## Pain Signals

- [ ] Mentions manual QA, regression testing, bugs, flaky tests, CI, or Playwright.
- [ ] Hiring QA automation or posting testing work.
- [ ] Recently launched or is preparing a release.
- [ ] Has AI outputs that need quality, safety, or grounding checks.

## Access Feasibility

- [ ] Can provide staging/test environment.
- [ ] Can provide test credentials or secure access method.
- [ ] Has at least one clear critical flow.
- [ ] Can define success criteria.

## Offer Match

- [ ] Audit first if problem is unclear.
- [ ] Framework first if they already know they need automation.
- [ ] AI audit first if they have chatbot/RAG/agent quality risk.
- [ ] Retainer first only if they already need ongoing QA support.

## Outreach Decision

Contact the lead only if at least 6 boxes are checked and one offer match is clear.
