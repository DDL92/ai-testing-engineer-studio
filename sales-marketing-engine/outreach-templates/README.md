# Outreach Templates

Use these templates for manual, personalized outreach.

## When To Use LinkedIn

- You know the decision-maker role.
- You can reference a product, post, launch, or hiring signal.
- You want to build a relationship before pitching.

## When To Use Upwork

- The client already posted a clear testing need.
- You can respond directly to scope, budget, and timeline.
- You can propose a small first milestone.

## When To Use Cold Email

- The company has a clear SaaS or AI product.
- There is a visible business reason for QA automation.
- You have a relevant personalization angle.

## Personalization Without Wasting Time

Use one concrete detail:

- Product type.
- Recent launch.
- Job post requirement.
- AI feature.
- Critical flow.
- Current QA pain.

## Daily Sending Limits

- LinkedIn connection requests: 5-10.
- LinkedIn first messages: 3-5.
- Upwork proposals: 3-5.
- Cold emails: 3-5.

## Follow-Up Rhythm

- Day 2: soft reminder.
- Day 5: clarify low-risk first step.
- Day 10: value-add message.
- Final: close the loop respectfully.
