## 5 Medium Proposals

### SaaS App

Hi, your project sounds like a good fit for a Playwright QA Automation Audit. I would review the critical SaaS flows, identify automation gaps, define smoke scenarios, and implement starter tests when access allows. This helps reduce manual regression time without overbuilding a large suite upfront.

### AI App

Hi, I can help validate your AI workflow beyond basic happy paths. I would check response relevance, grounding, format compliance, hallucination risk, and defensive prompt injection behavior. The output would be an audit report, reusable test matrix, and regression plan.

### E-Commerce

Hi, I can build maintainable Playwright coverage for the flows that directly affect revenue: login, product discovery, cart, checkout, and API health. I use TypeScript, POM, stable locators, HTML reports, and failure artifacts to make the suite easier to maintain.

### Software Agency

Hi, I work with teams that need reliable QA automation capacity without hiring full-time. I can create Playwright frameworks, audit client apps, improve regression coverage, and provide clean handoff documentation for your developers or QA team.

### Startup CTO

Hi, for a fast-moving startup, I would avoid a heavy framework at the start. I recommend identifying your highest-risk flows, adding CI-ready smoke coverage, and leaving your team with a simple Playwright structure that can grow safely.
