# Follow-Up Sequences

## Rules

- Follow up manually.
- Personalize each message.
- Stop after 2-3 follow-ups unless they respond.
- Do not guilt, pressure, or spam.

## Sequence For New Outreach

### Day 0

Send first personalized message.

### Day 2

Hi [Name], quick follow-up. The smallest useful starting point would be a fixed-scope QA automation audit: critical flows, automation gaps, and a practical roadmap. No large framework commitment needed.

### Day 5

Hi [Name], I will close the loop here. If regression risk or Playwright automation becomes a priority, I can help with a focused audit or starter framework.

## Sequence After Proposal Sent

Hi [Name], checking whether the proposal scope matches what you had in mind. I can adjust the first milestone smaller if you want to start with the audit only.

### Day 4
