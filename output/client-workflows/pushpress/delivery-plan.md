# Delivery Plan

## Week 1

- Confirm monthly scope, approved workflows, and evidence handling.
- Review existing QA assets and current release risks.
- Prioritize first smoke/regression targets.

## Week 2

- Maintain or create approved smoke coverage.
- Review failures and document risk evidence.
- Prepare concise progress notes.

## Week 3

- Expand coverage for one agreed high-value flow.
- Clean up flaky checks and improve reporting.
- Review upcoming release risks.

## Week 4

- Deliver monthly QA summary.
- Recommend next coverage priorities.
- Prepare renewal or next-month scope discussion.
