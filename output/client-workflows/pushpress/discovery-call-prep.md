2. How do you currently validate releases before they go live?
3. Which regressions have caused the most support load or engineering rework?
4. Do you already have any automated tests, and where are they unreliable or missing?
5. How often do you deploy product changes?
6. Which environments are safe for non-destructive QA review?
7. Are there public flows that can be reviewed without credentials?
8. Which flows require staging or a safe test account?
9. What should not be tested without explicit approval?
10. What would make a small QA audit clearly useful for the team?

13. Which payment-adjacent paths can be reviewed without using real payment data?
14. Which mobile viewport or device class matters most for core flows?
15. Where do users most often drop off or get blocked during onboarding?
